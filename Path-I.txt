**Path-I** is a free ChatGPT-like app built with **Streamlit** and **Hugging Face API**.  
It allows users to chat with an AI model in real-time with a clean and interactive interface.

---

## **Features**

- Free text-generation AI using Hugging Face (`EleutherAI/gpt-neo-125M`)  
- Polished chat interface with **chat bubbles**  
- Multi-message chat history maintained  
- Fully deployable on **Streamlit Cloud**  
- Custom **logo displayed at the top** of the app
